FREIGHT SHIPMENTS
By default, combined shipments or ANY order heavier than 70LBS will be sent out using MOTOR FREIGHT / default freight company chosen by us unless a freight company is provided by the BUYER. We will advise ALL customers before using freight as we understand that freight can cause a delay in shipment. If you insist, or for any other reason would not like the item shipped by freight, please contact us or respond to our inquiry so that we can accommodate your request.
